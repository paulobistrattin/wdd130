The Rafting Giants

Our mission is to provide unforgettable, safe and amazing white water adventures for families, friends and adventure seekers. Nature beauty + hospitality + expert guidance.

History

Founded in 2026 by a group of passionate river guides, The Rafting Giants started with just two rafts and a dream to share the wild rivers of our region with the world.

We love these rivers, and we want to keep them wild. That's why we're dedicated to protecting our local waterways and supporting the communities around them, so future generations can experience the same magic we do today.
